FFmpeg Windows Builds
----------------------
ClipWave downloads pre-compiled FFmpeg binaries for Windows from:
Website: https://www.gyan.dev/ffmpeg/builds/
Provided by: Gyan Doshi
These are official FFmpeg builds compiled for Windows.

---

ClipWave downloads and uses pre-compiled FFmpeg binaries as separate
executables. FFmpeg is not statically linked into the ClipWave application.
